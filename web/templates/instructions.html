What's this?

You will be asked to sort a set of images into groups of similar images.

Sorting

Click and drag images from the "Next Image" box into a group on the screen.

Adding Groups

Click "Add Group" on the left to create a new group. You can divide the images between as many as 8 groups.

Mechanical Turking

Once you have placed the last image from the "Next Image" box on the board, a "Finished" screen will be shown. You can copy the trial ID shown here into your Mechanical Turk form in order to be paid for your work.
