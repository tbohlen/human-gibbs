What's this?

You will be asked to sort a set of images into groups of similar images. They will be abstract, pixelated, black-and-white images.

Sorting

Click and drag images from the "Next Image" box into a group on the screen.

Adding Groups

Click "Add Group" on the left to create a new group. You can divide the images between as many as 8 groups. Once you place an image in a group, you may not move it again.
